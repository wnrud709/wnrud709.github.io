Fluid Dynamics Simulation

Summary

To simulate 2-D fluid dynamics of incompressible gases using Navier-Stokes equations. We will use GLSL fragment shaders.

Team Members

JuKyung Choi, Hye Min Cho

Problem Description

Solving this problem important because it provides us a way to render fluid motion which has been impossible to do only with our knowledge of meshes and raytracing. It would be our first time implementing a non-trivial shader. Brushing up on fluid physics would be another challenge for us.

We plan to present a simulation of fluid propagating in another homogenous fluid.

We hope to be able to take into account the different properties of fluid interacting and display different types of fluid propagating in an environment as a simulation. We will measure the quality by comparing the simulation to what we expect in the real world.

Schedule

  • Week 1: Figure out how to use opengl and how to apply Navier-Stokes to our problem. Set up a basic framework.
  • Week 2: Apply the physics equations and implement the simplest case of gas diffusion.
  • Week 3: Get a more complete simulation of gas behavior that incorporates a variety of factors like momentum, density, pressure, and stress.
  • Week 4: Prepare demos and presentation.
